{# #}{# #}{# #}Call for participation in capturing clinical trial information with CTrO-Editor.

If you are a medical student in the last years of study, or if you are familiar with clinical trial publications, this may be of your interest.

In the Semantic Computing Group at the Bielefeld University we are developing web tools and machine learning methods to help health practitioners in decision-making based on clinical evidence, and also in the support of living systematic reviews (i.e., reviews that are constantly updated). We have developed the CTrO-Editor tool to manually capture information from clinical trial publications and export it into a semantic-machine-readable format (RDF) that can be inserted into a knowledge base. To evaluate CtrO-Editor, we are asking for your kind participation on the following task:

Task:

  • Capture one clinical trial with our tool and give us feedback about it.
  • As a thank you for your time and effort, you will be considered in a raffle for a Thalia voucher of 100 Euros.

If you would like to participate:

  • If interested, contact Olivia Sanchez to get a clinical trial and an account to access CTrO-Editor.
